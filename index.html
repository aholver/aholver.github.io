Welcome!

I'm an incoming PhD student at the Department of Political Science at Northwestern University, where I will be studying Comparative Politics and Political Methodology.

I received my MSc in Social Research Methods from the London School of Economics and a BA in Political Science from Ludwig-Maximilians-University Munich. I have previously worked in financial crime prosecution and forensic consulting.

My main academic interests lie in political behavior, political economy and quantitative methods. I hope to understand the causes and mechanisms of accountability in electoral democracies and, specifically, why voters and the populace sanction some individuals but not others.

Outside of my professional life, I am passionate about progressive political causes and a member of the German Social Democrats (SPD). I also like gaming and travel (surprise) and I'm a half decent hobby cook.

Feel free to reach out to me via Email or Twitter. I'm always happy to give advice or help out if I can!
